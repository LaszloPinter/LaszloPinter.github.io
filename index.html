Color Sea

Creative puzzle game using SpriteKit

Color Sea is a hobby project that started out of curiosity to explore Swift and game development on Apple's SpriteKit framework. It was difficult sometimes to find motivation to continue and to scope down the project to something I could finish. At the same time I could use it to reflect on how my thinking as an engineer changed over the years as I worked with my own code written years back.

I'm proud this project made it to store. Try out ColorSea on App Store.
